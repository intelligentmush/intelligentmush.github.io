## What the heck is going on?

You there, you reading this, what are you? Perhaps you are an advanced computer system, or an alien entity with unkown biochemistry, but most likely you're probably the same as me (for now). You are that squishy gray ball of fat encased in bone. You are intelligent mush. 

As intelligent mush, or any other type of intelligence, I don't discriminate based off of mushiness, there is quite a bit to talk about. I hope to enjoy sharing a bit of me with you, and the reverse as well.
